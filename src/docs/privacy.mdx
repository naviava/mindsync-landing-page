# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: December 10, 2025_

Mindsync (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy.

By using our Website, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

We collect information in the following ways:

This includes:

- Contact details (name, email, phone number)
- Messages submitted through contact forms
- Information shared during service inquiries or consultations
- Any files or documents voluntarily uploaded or shared with us

### **1.2 Automatically Collected Information**

When you browse our Website, we may collect:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring URLs
- General usage statistics

We use these insights to improve performance, security, and user experience.

### **1.3 Cookies & Tracking Technologies**

We use cookies and similar tools to:

- Maintain essential website functionality
- Improve loading performance
- Analyze site traffic and usage
- Measure marketing or campaign performance

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Respond to inquiries or communicate with you

- Improve website design, speed, and security
- Analyze how visitors use our Website
- Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access
- Comply with legal or regulatory obligations

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)
